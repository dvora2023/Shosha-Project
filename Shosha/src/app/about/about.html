About Shosha

This website was created after the passing of my beloved grandmother Shoshana - and it is also named after her.

My grandmother's greatest love was the Sabbath.

Her respect and reverence for the Sabbath were truly exceptional.

She would start preparing at the beginning of the week, and the highlight was the Sabbath table.

The table, which hosted no less than 30 guests at each meal, was already set by Thursday.

Every detail was carefully designed - from the tablecloth and the runner above it to the personal name cards for each guest - everything was arranged with beauty and elegance.

She had many sets of everything and never repeated the same design twice.

She showed us what true inspiration, creativity and respect for the Sabbath looked like.

All the photos here on the site are of her personal tables.

May the zechus of this project and all the chizzuk it generates be l'iluy nishmas:

Shoshana bas R' Naftali ע"ה
